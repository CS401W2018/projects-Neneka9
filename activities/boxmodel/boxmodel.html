What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

Block tag has 100% width. The height is as height as the content. Those can change the size with CSS. (Ex. Div, header, nav, footer, main, h1-6, p, UL, OL, Li, etc)

The space of Inline tag is just around the text (Ex. Span, em, strong, b, I, a, img, etc)

What is the margin, border, and padding?

Margin is the outside (around) box (how much space you want around the box)

Padding is the inside of the box (how much space you want between text and border)

Border is a line around the text

What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

Sized: px, thin, in, cm

relative: 50%, 0.5 em

When would you use a figure tag?

causes break line (Ex. to put the image between the text)
