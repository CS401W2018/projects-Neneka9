Island of Hawaii is the biggest island in Hawaii.

If you love nature, just go!

Common actitities and places to visit:

  • active Volcanos (Kilauea, Mauna Loa)
  • Stargazing (Mauna Kea)
  • Colorful Beaches (Punalu'u Black Sand Beach, Papakolea green sand Beach)
  • History (King Kamehameha I, Kapaau, Puako)
  • Waterfalls (Akaka Falls, Rainbow Falls)
  • Wildlife (Hawaiian green sea turtle, Nene goose, Hawaiian monk seal)
