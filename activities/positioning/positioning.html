What is normal flow in HTML?

Header, H1 tag, nav, main, footer

Normally, those tags don't share their space.

Ex. The lines of paragraph tag and img tag are not shared.

What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?

Float is to make the element to share the space with other element.

You need to clear because everything comes into the space.

3. What do absolute, fixed, and relative do?

Change the position of elements (ex. Img or paragraph)

Relative - slide it around (it could be on the paragraph or other elements)

Absolute - when scrolling, it moves along with page

Fixed - when scrolling, it stays on the screen

4. How can you make nice navigation buttons for mobile devices?

Use "display: block;" with padding, boarder-bottom in CSS. If you want to move those buttons, you can use "float"
